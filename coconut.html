Coconut

We AACO Export and Import an unit of AACO Enterprises offering one of the products of Coconut which are procured from Tamilnadu, Kerala India. These are offered to clients at fair costs and are supplied from reputable producers.

We AACO Export and Import, are recognised as one of the top exporters and suppliers of coconut. Due to our coconut's superior quality and higher water content, our selection of coconuts is generally considered good by our cherished customers. We offer fresh, husked and semi-husked coconuts. Before choosing to pack them, we separate our coconuts into easy-to-crack varieties and those with excess water. Coconuts of every kind come from trees that are cultivated naturally. We take care to keep our organic, fresh coconuts intact while packing them.

Key Points to be Noted from AACO Export and Import are, AACO is one of the wholesalers of fresh husked coconut suppliers and has expanded the venture to various parts of Globe. The coconuts provided by us are packed from farms directly. We follow the standards and do not compromise the high quality of our products. These coconuts are packed properly ensuring long shelf-life.
