Cocopeat

WWe AACO Export and Import an unit of AACO Enterprises offering one of the products of Coco Peat. These are offered to clients at fair costs and are supplied from reputable producers.

Blocks of cocopeat are regarded as the best growing medium. The resulting cocopeat powder is sieved, cleaned, dried, and then compacted into blocks. The blocks can be utilized for a variety of planting needs. Natural rooting hormones and anti-fungal qualities of coir are well known. The cocopeat blocks are packaged safely as well. Stretch wrap and straps are used to secure the blocks. On the customer's request, we may also alter the packaging. They often come in small bales of five- and ten-kilograms blocks, as well as 650 grammes each brick. Leading coir peat suppliers in India with a stellar reputation are AACO Export and Import.

We AACO Export and Import, export and supply coco peat, coir pith, and coco peat blocks and bricks. We provide 650gm Coco Peat Blocks, 5kg Blocks, and Coco Peat Grow Bags. We are one of India's top exporters of coconut coir pith. Coir Peat's aerated structure makes it useful for farming. Due to the product's ability to absorb water and its superior soil conditioner qualities, farming is one of its main applications. The dried and processed coco peat is used to make a variety of products, including blocks, briquettes, tablets, etc.
